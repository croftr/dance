The Knowsley School of Dance was established in Knowsley Village in 1953.

Tuition includes The Royal Academy of Dance Ballet Examination. We are a fully established memeber of the International Dance Training Association.

We take beginners from the age of 3 and all pupils are trained for International Dance Teacher Association examinations in:

  • Tap
  • Theatre
  • Modern Dance
  • Gymnastic Dance
  • Ballet

Annual Show Production at Crosby Civic Hall.

All pupils will be tutired by the Principal Harriett Croft (R.A.D. Memeber, I.D.T.A Fellowship) who is assistted by fully qualified teachers.

Principal Harriet Croft was awarded with The Presidents Award.
Given to teachers who have for many years dedicated themselves to the service of The Royal Academy of Dance over and above the call of duty
